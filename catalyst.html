Formerly a human studying <i>Japanese Literature</i> at Kamii University's Department of Literature</i>, he was transplanted with <a href="http://tokyoghoul.wikia.com/wiki/Rize_Kamishiro">Rize</a>'s kakuhou and became a <b>One-Eyed Ghoul.</b> After joining <a href="http://tokyoghoul.wikia.com/wiki/Anteiku">Anteiku</a> as a part-time waiter, he learns how to live as a Ghoul and became known as <i>Eyepatch (眼帯)</i>.
